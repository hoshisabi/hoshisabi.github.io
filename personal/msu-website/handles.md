## Sabby

Sabby is short for Sebastian Trump. It is also my current favored handle.

It came from a story in the "Devils and Demons" anthology. "The Art of Outrage, or the Philosophy of Sebastian Trump." It was a tremendously influential story ... at least for a short period of my life. At the time, I was changing handles faster than I changed my clothes. On a friend of mine's BBS (Doogie Howser is his current handle) he would change my handle, I would change his, and so on. We came up with some ridiculous ones. Well, I finally changed it to this and it stuck in my head... but not on the board. (Strangely enough, he still goes by the handle that I dubbed him — a purely whimsical handle. Doogie Howser was on, I changed his handle, and he left it... for about 5 or more years now.)

When I moved to Lansing, I wanted to log onto a BBS that Kelly was not allowing me to. Well, I had to disguise myself somehow, so I adopted an entirely different personality, typing style, and handle to match. I picked something that I knew I could "act like." Thus, Sebastian Trump. Needless to say, it got me in much trouble, but the handle stuck. Somehow, I earned a measure of respect with that persona. In fact, it started to merge with my current online persona. Life is weird.

## Glorfindle the Blue

As most computer geeks, I fancied and still fancy myself as something of a writer. Glorfindle the Blue was a wizard in one of my stories. Glorfindle is awfully similar to Glorfindel (from Tolkien) — but they're not related. I never did finish the story on paper, but I know it in my head. For a 13 year old, I had thought out an awfully grim story.

When I logged onto my first BBS, I needed to think of a handle. I wanted to pick something that I could remember, and it's the first thing that popped into my mind. My dad's friend who was showing me the BBSs (on my new 300 bps modem) was pressing me for time. No one believed that I would remember it. Shortly thereafter, when I got my 1200, I used no other handles.

## Constantine

This is the easiest. It's a straight rip-off of John Constantine, my favorite character from Swamp Thing.

My first character on my first mud was named (guess) Glorfindle. I tired of that character, since he kept dying, so I abandoned him, and became a female character (all the better to get more equipment) named "Swampy." Friend of mine wizzed not too long after that and did me the "favor" of correcting my gender. I restarted as a new character named "Constantine" since Swampy confused too many people ("But I thought you were a girl?! Says here you're a guy now.")

## Others

Some I would be embarrassed to admit to. I've used Arcadion Black (another character in the Glorfindle story), Some Guy in the Street, The Guy your Mother Warned you Of, Open Flesh Wound, Me, All, Anonymous, and any number of other joke handles. Like all 13 year old computer geeks, I went through a period of changing my handle every week.
