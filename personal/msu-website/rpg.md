# Gaming Geek Paradise

*Archived from http://www.msu.edu/~chapmand/rpg.html (captured Feb 18, 2005 via Wayback Machine)*

---

Yeh, you're cool. You collect those games that no one else collects. When people wanna know about a new RPG that's just been released, they come to you first. You're a gaming geek.

Nothing against AD&D, but there's already tons of information on how to get AD&D out there. You can find just about anything by looking on the web. But, some games aren't blessed with as much availability. My favorites, Ars Magica and Shadowrun, are amongst those. Ars Magica is particularly hard to search for, as the names pop up as substrings in all the search engines.

The way I see it, we've all got at least two or three gaps in every one of our collections. Some of us are just starting out and need to get them all. So, I devote this page to people running into the same problems I had when I started out.

Some hints for your search: Be patient. Unless you need the book for the next gaming session, you should keep looking to see if you find a better price somewhere else. You usually will. Don't obsess about the price, however. Sometimes a higher price on an item is a blessing for a collector, because it means that the item won't be snagged as quick — leaving it there for your grubby hands to pick up.
