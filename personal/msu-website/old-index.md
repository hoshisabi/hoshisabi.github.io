I wish I weren't dchapman here, then I could hide underneath a pseudonym and leave my real name concealed. As it is, I was considering hiding beneath my real name, and leaving my pseudonym concealed. Then I realized that was silly... so, I am also known as [Sabby](handles.md#sabby), [Glorfindle the Blue](handles.md#glorfindle-the-blue), [Constantine](handles.md#constantine), and [some other ones](handles.md#others) which fell into disuse or no one can or will remember.

I'm a programmer for MEDSTAT, programming in FORTRAN 77. Sometimes I waste my time programming other things in C, Pascal, and Assembly. What do I program? I have no idea... At work, I make programs that do things to big files and then make them different big files. Maybe some time I'll put up some sample source I've worked on.

My life isn't that confusing. I live in a small apartment. I have no idea what I do there. But I live there. I have a daughter and a girlfriend. My girlfriend could also have a web-page of her own, but she hasn't bothered to remake it yet (it died when the server crashed.) I may get bored one day, make one for her, and sneak it back on.

I'm a wizard on a couple of muds. I'll link them to my page.

Someday I may have more entries on my page. Of course, some day I might pay off all my bills.

By the way, this page has been included as one of: **King Craig's Bottom 95% of Web Sites**
