### Lamorna & The Getaway Gang

- Azrael illusioned the rest of the party to sneak up to the lake.
- Made it across to the middle of the lake.
- A bowl on the island had coals — magic leaped from the bowl to reveal the horse. Myster leaped to it.
- The horse/unicorn greeted the party after a delay, treating them as outsiders.
- The unicorn's name is **Lamorna** (her mate is **Elidon**).
- She explains the lake is a portal, and that the League would be looking for her husband.
- Told the party of a guide that could lead them from Yon to the **Palace of Heart's Desire**.
- During Zybilna's reign, no one could enter the palace without her invitation. Lamorna doubts the cover has that same influence now.
- Inside the palace lives the **Jabberwock** — she suggests the party avoid it.
- Azrael asked about Will. She has not run into him but says he is a complicated individual. He is wanted for "stealing" the children (i.e., freeing them).
- A person in a boat was **Zargash**, a Priest of Orcus. The party killed him. He is now a specter.
- **Warduke** protects **Kelek** (who wants to take the stuff of his enemies). Ringlerun and Valoris are also involved.
- **Scylla** is there too — a warlock of Baba Yaga.
- The party left toward the **Getaway Gang**.
- Will confronted them when they arrived; another kid hid. Dawn offered cookies.
- Saw **Red** (Will) with them. He says the Getaway Gang is real.
- He has a plan to break the kids free from **Loomlurch**, where someone holds the children hostage.
- The children are guarded by tin soldiers, held captive in a workshop, and haunted by **boggles** — oily creatures from nightmare.
- As a show of good faith, the party can rescue some kids taken by a redcap named **Big Red**.
- *We broke here.*
