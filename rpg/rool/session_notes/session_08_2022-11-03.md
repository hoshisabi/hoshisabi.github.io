### Freeing Sir Talavar & Downfall

- Picked up after a skip. Talked to the hill about tracking down the creature in the tunnels.
- *"Let's give this mountain an acid enema." "Who's taking enema and who's taking point."*
- Fought Meenlocks (with lair action) who were breaking keys. Lair actions occurred during the fight.
- Returned the key to Jingle Jangle, who helped free **Sir Talavar**. Sir Talavar gave the party a +1 dagger with *Cast Heroism*.
- Left on the water in a raft.
- Saw a vision in the water around the raft: a headless child-sized scarecrow trying on new heads of random things, finally settling on a large gourd.
- Gloop saw rows of severed Bullywug heads, all impaled on spikes and chattering with each other.
- In Downfall, vision is reduced due to fog.
- Saw Bullywugs. Introduced themselves and were told to speak to the King. *"Ufgunk sends his regards."* Myster disguised as a Bullywug.
- Ran into the balloon factory, which was on fire. Little alive coals were setting fires. Myster, claiming to be Ufgunk, put out the fire in the balloon factory.
- Puke [?], who ran the factory, explained that Wiggle Wog and Mongort stole casks from Bavlorna, and now they've set fire to the factory. Mongort is in jail.
- The Great Croaker is the Bullywug god.
- Came upon the chattering heads from Gloop's vision. One of them — **Clapperclaw** — could lead them out of the realm, but he lost his stag head. If they get his head back he could help them escape.
- The wall of heads were all former rulers; all agreed that King Gollumin is unworthy.
- Ended here.
