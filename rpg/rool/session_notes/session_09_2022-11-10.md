### King Gollup & Bavlorna's Cottage

- Met with King Gollup. Alex portrayed himself as a great prophet of the Great Croaker.
- Passed a secret note (in Sylvan, with a frog boot symbol): *"Find Illig, the Baron of Muckstump, at the race. The revolution lives!"*
- King Gollup has a baby crocodile named **Snoodle**.
- Clothing Shop / Haberdashery run by Dumphry Froggart.
- Directed to rebels who had a plan to stab the king in the eye (by tripping him), become king themselves, then give Bavlorna's crates to her. They felt the king was weak and the Great Croaker was superstitious.
- Found a box with Bavlorna's name on the lid — inside were several animal carcasses.
- Returned to the King. Series of events: Myster convinced the King to bow to the Great Croaker, who was then killed/replaced by Illig, who was in turn deposed in favour of democracy.
- Human-sized doll pincushions. Door leading out of the rear. Cabinet/cupboard with a silver Hourglass sigil — can be opened by pushing/rotating it. Teacup, ceramic [items].
- Inside: a cauldron with a note saying "Spittlespew," and lots of miscellaneous junk and goodies.
- Upstairs: found a giant wasp nest. Led the frogs from downstairs up to eat the wasps.
- Frogs on the balcony. Saw **Bavlorna** upstairs with another individual.
- Found a redcap — the chief, named **BH Bloodytoes** — who was chasing vultures. Convinced the vultures to leave.
- Bloodytoes is allergic to anyone running widdershins near her (counterclockwise).
- Left off there.
