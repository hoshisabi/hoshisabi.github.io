### Yarspinner's Library & Nobody Revealed

*Returned after an absence. Recap: did not trust "Nobody," but were willing to explore as "rude guests without necessarily getting into shit."*

- Yarspinner's library contains fey-realm parody titles, including:
  - *Self-Help for Wizards*
  - *Adventure: Star-Crossed Lovers* / *Frankenstein's Brain*
  - *Dewey the Bear* (the Bear)
  - *Goldilocks Calls the Bear*
  - *Where the Feywild Things Are*
  - *The Boy Who Sped Through the Thorns* (he whistled the whetstone and ran backwards)
  - Classics section: *Carlotta's Web*
  - *Lord of the Faerie Rings*
- Yarspinner probably went through the secret door in the Classics section.
- Task in Classics: find an unlit midnight candle where the campestri's signature is.
- Went through the secret passage. Found:
  - A book that talked about Myster [Bird?]
  - A book about Dawn and the cupcake contest
  - A book about Rool and the snail that saved him
- Saw Nobody pick up a book. Nobody destroyed the *Tale of Strongheart*.
- After reading the books they regained their memories.
- Chased after Nobody — he turned into a racing snail to escape.
- Yarspinner caught them. They fought Nobody / Yarspinner. Myster threatened to destroy the books.
- Yarspinner agreed to let them go if they gave him a tale he didn't already know.
- Chris told Yarspinner the story of how he [Chris] got created. (31-letter performance from Chris.) Yarspinner mulled it over, accepted it — and the mist dispersed.
- Cast Hold Person on Nobody — revealed to be **Zardu**, a half-orc assassin from the Strongheart tale [the League of Malevolence agent]. They bashed his brains in.
- The **real Yarspinner** came back and apologised. Gave the party *Slippers of Spiderclimb*. Explained that his domain had merged with Prismeer and he is working on getting unmerged to free from the corruption.
- Levelled up.

**Item received — Midnight Candle** *(Wondrous Item, common):* While holding this candle you can cause a harmless flame to appear on its wick, shedding bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet. When you cause the flame to appear, you may designate up to ten creatures who are able to see the light from it.
