### Cauldron Movement & The Frozen Dwarf

- Went into the ballroom and noticed the cauldron had been moved.
- Instead of the cauldron, we found the three flying devices of Bavlorna, Skabatha, and Endelyn.
- A voice mocked us from the walls, saying "You think of yourselves as heroes."
- Invisible Derad jammed the horn into the forehead of a creature, turning it back into a unicorn.
- Found a room filled with weapons that compliment their users. Recovered a scimitar and a weapon for Eulyon, among others.
- Dawn, Mystar, and Sebastian explored another room. They found a frozen dwarf scholar, a document replying to Rotten Ethel, and a deal with Azreal.
- Visited the library and met a hunchback man who informed us that the library was closed.
- Checked the sludge tower and found caged figures holding wands.
- Discovered an owl in a cage. Chose not to free her.
- Entered another room containing giant cacti and an abundance of food.
- Discovered a musical instrument and heard a clinking sound. Followed the sound to a state room (glass statue room).
- Notes on monsters/teleportation:
  - Hobgoblin = Goh
  - Harpy = Bah (would teleport, so we will come back here)
  - Ogre = Bah
  - Bugbear = Yah
- Went into the next room and found a bowl. Azreal became trapped behind the surface of the water as if it were glass.
- Peering behind the water, we saw a thin dwarf face and spooky armor.
- Floated past a room containing 8 wooden mannequins dressed as Tasha at various ages / Witch Queen of Perrenland.
- In the next room, discovered cradles with filthy cloths inside. A clawed creature reached forward, with symbols hidden from view. These were dretches; the baby dretches represented the suppressing of Tasha's negative emotions.
- Went around the corner and found a secret library.
- Discovered a balcony looking over the sludge room, where a high elf noble was holding a crystal globe of water. A frozen creature was attempting to clean.
- Forced a doorway that was meant to require a password. Engaged in a philosophical discussion and left some yarn.
- Continued further and discovered a laboratory. Recovered:
  - 1 Potion of Growth
  - 3 Potions of Healing
  - 2 Potions of Hill Giant Strength
  - 3 Bean pods that can be hurled for *Plant Growth*
- Found a three-tiered mahogany table with 3 gleaming jars, each wreathed in mist. Each jar contained Tasha at different ages.
- Entered a marble hall containing a mirror.
- Found a bedroom with an obsidian chess set and a bloodstone-colored set, with two lamyas lounging on the bed.
