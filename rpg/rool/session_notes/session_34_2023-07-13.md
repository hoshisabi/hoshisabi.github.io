### The Mill, the Giant & Mudlump's Romantic Mission

*Single page of notes.*

- Took a long rest.
- Myster wrote a letter and sent it secretly. (Unknown to most of the party.)
- Fought a **dark mantle** — put it into lightning and it fled. Several skill checks.
- Made it to **the mill**. Inside: many packages. People opened them — contents included alchemy jars, a bad doll, a bag of magical beans, and other traps.
- Went into the next room: mattress, wardrobe, mirror. A door was locked — seemed like it would lead outside.
- The wardrobe held various clothes sized for a large bipedal creature (a giant).
- A mirror made Myster look better.
- A **picture book**: *"The Mouse, Snake and Me."*
- **Letters with boots** — a note reading: *"Sweet Boy — [I've had the] darklings leave you a gift. Crank a day to keep the [darklings] away. Hugs & teeth. Claws & Kisses."* [A hag's affectionate letter to her giant companion.]
- A door behind a combination lock: **1234**. Magic stairs teleported the party upstairs.
- A **giant** upstairs, mourning over a dead giant crone.
- Azrael tried to crank a mechanism the other way — could not do it. The giant shouted at Azrael in Giant.
- Convinced the giant that they were sent by Endelyn and that she needed to turn the crank the other way.
- Arranged to have **Mudlump** [the cyclops from session 28, who asked about love] come meet her — a romantic mission.
