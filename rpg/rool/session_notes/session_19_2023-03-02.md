### The Neverlanding Ship & Big Red

- Went onto the **Neverlanding Ship**.
- Saw a redcap scrubbing the deck — he threatened the party, but Chris talked him into letting them onboard. His name was **Saucantish**.
- He led them to **Big Red**, who had a hook for a hand. Messy cabin.
- Big Red denied having the kids. He wanted the party to kill an alligator and tell him the location of the Moogin crocodile.
- The party agreed to talk to the alligator instead — then got attacked by the crocodile. Chris released the croc to the main deck.
- The crocodile ate Big Red, who dropped his keys. Got the key.
- Freed three captives: an oil can, a pixie (**Bela**), and a displacer kitten. Also freed **Squirt** and **Star**.
- Left the ship and found a cart with awnings — **"Gordlin Dibs Fine Confections."**
- A pair of goblins poked their heads out. Traded a Goodberry for candy, curious what the candy might do. They are set up in the goblin market in Loomlurch.
- Concerned about **Chucklehead**: the goblins say his hand went stiff ever since his head was replaced with a toffee apple.
- They sell treats like beetles, ants, and worms.
- Chucklehead has had a crisis of faith — gone soft, wants to make candy and protect the children.
- Attacked by a mound of flowers (**shambling mound**). Defeated it; individual flowers popped out.
- They dropped an **amulet of lucky numbers**.
- *Ended it here.*
