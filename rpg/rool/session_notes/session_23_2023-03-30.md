### Loomlurch: The Dragon in the Oven & Owlbert is Born

- Found a **Box of Baby Teeth** — an item that allows you to be reminded of your dreams.
- Found **Sowpig**, a ghoul girl, in a cocoon.
- Found portraits of svelte women and four hags. Identified **Bavlorna**, **Skabitha**, and a svelte woman who matched a sculpture outside the Witchlight Carnival — likely **Tasha/Iggwilv**. The figures all shift in their frames.
- Sebastian got pricked by a thorn. His face — and the faces of his siblings — appeared in the frames.
- Myster went ahead and stealthed to find the kids. One was the goblin kid they were looking for. Found a spooky doll.
- That kid warned the party not to trust a kid named **"Pod"** that they had already rescued.
- Found a young human shackled to a table, peeling potatoes. A gruff male adult voice came from beneath a grate.
- Azrael accidentally woke a **green dragon named Candlefall** from inside the oven. Myster hit it with an "Eldritch Snoot Boop" and then pretended to be [something harmless] to convince it to stand down.
- **Mishka** knows the secret password: **"Rumple Clump."**
- Freed a dwarf and a fire elemental.
- Found a **lost thing: Mantle of the Forest Guardian** — a cloak that adds a bonus on concentration checks and allows self-polymorph into a **Griffin**.
- Found letters/mechanical birds in an **aviary** — talking about the unicorn horn.
- Came back to the owlbear cart. **Juniper** was agitated — her egg had hatched while she was held. She broke free. The owlbear baby is named **Owlbert**.
- The Jabberwock was mentioned.
- **Skabitha** came by at the end and laughed at her sister's misfortune.
- The dwarf's name was **Elkhorn** (League of Malevolence fighter); **Ringlerun** (the wizard) was also mentioned.
