### Brigganock Mines, Molliver & the Purple Worm's Eyes

- Woke up from Revel. The Korreds had made statues while the party slept. **Argantle** (Korred queen) revealed there is a **secret tunnel into Motherhorn**.
- The **Mantle of the Forest Guardian** gained new abilities: speak while in animal form, and polymorph into an owlbear (in addition to the griffin from session 23). This was because the party left the menagerie to be safe with **Squeaky Squeaker**.
- The party was asked to bring statues out in front of the Korred cave.
- A **cyclops** came and angrily demanded [something] back, in Giantish. Myster explained that **Pollinella** (the bee) was in love with Amidor. The cyclops asked: *"What is love?"* His name was **Mudlump**; he asked if Myster could help him find love. Pollinella says he wasn't a bad keeper.
- Approached the **Brigganock mines**. Placed statues in front of the mines.
- Went inside — the sound of pickaxes caused **Dawn** to fall asleep. Could not wake her up.
- Brigganocks explained and talked to Myster. When caught, they revealed: since the party was going after **Creeping Lynn** (Endelyn), they want to help. They had taken [something] from Lynn.
- There was a **human** in the Brigganock group — named **Molliver**. The brigganocks think the Korreds are working with Endelyn.
- To make peace the party would have to talk to **Obud**, who lives across the cavern.
- Found a **100-foot gap** across the cavern — spanned by a truss bridge. Purple lights lined the way.
- A brigganock gave **Azrael** a **wishstone** — it was the wish from the Witchlight Carnival. [Azrael's lost wish recovered!]
- Brigganocks warned that the cave was guarded by a fearsome beast that sounded like a **purple worm**.
- The party managed to crack its googly eyes.
- *Ended here.*
