### The Helm of Telepathy & The Balloon Raid

- Room had a frog-shaped porcelain jar with chicken legs, and a helmet on a wooden mannequin.
- Myster took the helmet off the statue and it attacked. Put his own hat on it instead and it stopped.
- The helmet is the **Helm of Telepathy**.
- Message from the wishbone about **Skabitha**: she forgets the first creature she sees when she wakes up. Her memory of it returns each night when she sleeps. If a creature is forgotten by her, that creature is invisible to her left [the whole house?].
- Apparently there is a competition between the Bullywug King (the Holy King), Baronessa the Lady of the Slime, and Croakersaldo the Over-Croaker. They are preparing to battle to the death at the proving grounds to decide who should be king.
- Myster appeared as the Great Croaker to stop the holy war.
- Went to the Bullywug prison. Released **Mugwort** and talked about stealing a balloon to attack the harengon Bandik.
- The serpent that killed Wiggle Wog may be the Jabberwock.
- Picked up **Snoodle** the baby alligator.
- Took the balloon to the harengon tree.
- Took out the guards. Found a logbook of who paid the toll — names: Hurley, Vansel, Octavian, Glister, Sir Talavar, [others].
- Fought and killed a bunch of harengon. Ended it after the first room.
