### The Korred Clans & the Basilisk's Stomachache

*(Took a long rest at the start.)*

- **Flint Clan**: Went to the Flint Korreds first. When they arrived, saw two Korreds fighting. Myster charmed them. Found out they were **sleep-deprived**.
  - They told the party to talk to **Budoc** (the leader). He said they needed to silence the brigganocks who were making noise that interrupted their sleep.
  - Searched and found it was little creatures called **mites** that enjoyed making irritating noises. Dawn made her own noise and led the mites into metal cages. Once covered, the mites went back to sleep.
- **Basalt Clan**: Saw Korreds including **Yana**, the leader. The ground rumbled.
  - She said that **Gorgoniad** and other Korred friends had gone off and had not returned.
  - Went to rescue them. Investigated a house and saw a map to the **Brigganock mines**.
  - Went there, found a river of lava. On an island, saw kids cowering from a pair of **salamanders** stalking them.
  - Turns out the salamanders were looking for their eggs — an obsidian clan had taken them. Returned the kids and the eggs.
  - Heard there was a **dragon shrine** laden with bloodstones and glittering gem stones.
- **Marble Clan**: Went to the Marble Korreds to seek peace. They declined to fight — they were artists and uninvolved. The kids dragged Dawn aside to talk about gathering materials.
- Worked with the kids to make a **puppet/statue that mocked Endelyn**. She was controlled like a marionette by the eclipse — did a "thriller dance" and [freed her from her parts?]. With that, they convinced the adults: if the kids could be inspired, they should be too.
- **Slate Clan**: When her mother passed away, **Myzilda** became leader of the Slate clan. She was on board with joining but unsure of her own ability.
  - A puzzle to earn an item. Myzilda saw an inscription: *you should always lead by example.* She gifted the **pendant** to the party to lead by example. The party gave it to **Jingle Jangle** since it can counter [harmful] effects.
- **Jagu** appeared with a look of concern. He explained they had been working on statues to put outside the **Brixemout mines**, but a **basilisk** had mistaken them for prey and had stomach issues from eating the statues. The party teamed up to help the basilisk empty its stomach.
- *Ended here. The party learned how to manipulate [marionettes/levers?] for the next step.*
