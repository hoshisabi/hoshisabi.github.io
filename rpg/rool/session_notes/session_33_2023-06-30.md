### Endelyn's Fortunes & Skylla's Last Stand

- Entered the **audience chamber** of Motherhorn.
- **Jingle Jangle** is now Endelyn's servant — she was sent here by Endelyn.
- **Endelyn** pulled the curtains back and offered to tell fortunes:
  - **Myster**: *"Beware the windows."*
  - **Azrael**: *"Stabbed through the heart by an invisible assassin. Next time: grander — a dragon carrying [you] into a storm."*
  - **Dawn**: *"[Someone] reads her thoughts while she eats meats, and [she is] buried/crushed by a falling castle."*
- Dawn tied her fortune to Jingle Jangle's [as leverage/threat].
- Sebastian + Myster discussed possibly giving up their shadows.
- Dawn threw an axe at Jingle Jangle.
- Endelyn said it was *interesting* and that *"everything has a price — she will see it paid up!"* Then disappeared.
- Went downstairs to find **Skylla**. Chaos. Skylla did not start her play — sat down and said: *"I deserved better."*
- **Chim** threw mummy dust on her.
- **Molliver**, **Amidor**, and others appeared and joined the fight.
- **Skylla died** and dropped her stuff.
- Went upstairs: found **lightning in a bottle** in one chamber, and **paper birds that were letters**.
- Found a **bell** that said *"Heart's Desire."* Myster rang it.
- Something large came slapping toward them — **cranes** appeared that could take the party to the **Palace of Heart's Desire**.
- The companions needed the party to open the **beacons** to let **Hurley** and **Glistor** through.
- The cranes need the party to **take care of the mist** first.
- *Done for the night.*
