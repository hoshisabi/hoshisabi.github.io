### Obud's Pony & The Moonstone Dragon

- Crossed the chasm. Found a piebald pony with a tiny hut on its back.
- Knocked on the door; a wizened brigganock came out — Obud, their leader [or similar]. Told us the purple worm was known as "Jerry" — it ate children. Obud was glad it was gone.
- Horse name unclear. Scholar Lyrint also mentioned.
- Mazinoth and Lyrint — the Moonstone [dragon?]. Became obsessed with the [Ela---] Star.
- Lyrinth made a deal with Endelyn. She went into an eternal slumber.
- Agreed to rescue the dragon in exchange for the brigganocks' help.
- Returned to the Korreds, told them why, convinced them [to work together — notes very faint].
- Headed north to find the astronomer's throne.
- Came upon a cowled figure with a cloaked wagon. He pulled back the curtain — the wagon has a painted backdrop of Yon.
- Marionettes dropped from the top of the set depicting the party. Scenes from Myster's point of view. Perspective shifted to Azrael's when Myster gave away his eye.
- A ticket appeared at the end: "Good for 1 private audience."
- Arrived at a cliff with a breathtaking view of the landscape.
- Traced the path of constellations with a telescope — the path forms the shape of a dragon, opening a portal. Heard a voice asking for help.
- When feet hit the ground inside, became lighter — reduced gravity in the demiplane.
- A gauth was inside. It asked what magic the party brought; Myster gave up the Discordant Harp.
- After fighting the gauth and destroying the crystals, released the dragon (Mazinoth), who can clear the skies and arrange an eclipse.
- Gave Mazinoth a guide and another item.
