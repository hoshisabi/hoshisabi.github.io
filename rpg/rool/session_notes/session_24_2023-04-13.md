### The Dread Shroud & Boggles at Little Oak

*Single page of notes.*

- **Loomlurch was on fire** as the party left. Heard a large angry creature in the background to the north — in the direction that would lead to Yon.
- Summoned a Giant Eagle. Azrael and Myster chased the creature down. Turned out it was the **Jabberwock**, chasing Will. Myster cast Psychic Lance and then the Jabberwock wisely fled.
- Myster seemed to be acting depressed. Azrael tried to strip-search him to figure it out. Myster was angry at first, but when he put the tunic back on he became apathetic again.
- Conversation didn't reveal much, but Will noticed that Myster's tunic was an odd color — different than a robe. It appeared a different color to everyone who looked at it. The spool had "**S.N.**" initials — **Skabitha Nightshade**.
- *Identify* revealed it as a **"Dread Shroud"** — the color of frustration and self-doubt.
- Returned to **Little Oak**. Wondered where all the kids had gone — they were back at the camp.
- Myster tried plucking a thread out of the cloak, but it went missing. A red balloon floated by hauntingly.
- Fought **boggles** who had kidnapped **Owlbert**, **Pod**, and others. Recovered them.
- *Ended it there.*
