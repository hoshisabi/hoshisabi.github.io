- Left toward Telemy Hill. Found huge footprints filled with water.
- Chris had a memory: Telemy Hill is mobile — those are the hill's footprints. Telemy Hill was large enough to follow easily.
- Greeted with the scent of sweet fruit. The trees lightly sway as the hill walks.
- Heard a voice greet them: "Hello friends." Didn't see anyone.
- Realized the trees were talking to them — the trees speak for the hill.
- The trees told them that **Jingle Jangle** the goblin can help — she collects keys. The voice passed from tree to tree as they walked.
- Jingle Jangle had not left her den for the better part of three days. The harengon have been coming into her home and absconding with her keys.
- The cave entrance is carved like a keyhole. Scent of iron in the air. A candle sputtering and keys everywhere.
- Jingle Jangle: *"I'm going to start fucking people up with a flail of eldritch padlocks."*
- Jingle Jangle is scared of locks — she was not treated nicely and was left in a cage by Bavlorna. Bavlorna "tried to help" — using her powers to grant wishes, she helped Jingle Jangle be less afraid of locks by giving her keys. She had the key [to Sir Talavar's cage] until the harengon stole it.
- If willing to stay and ambush the harengon, cook dinner.
- She asks for help with truffle hunting. Along the way, the trees ask if they could have Jingle Jangle let the hill know before she digs again.
- Suggested the hill could find companions to speak for it indoors. Jingle Jangle explained she hadn't been digging — it may have been the harengon.
- Chris gave the Everchanging Key trinket in exchange for knowledge about the flail [of eldritch padlocks].
- Set up several extra blankets for the night.
- In the night, rabbit ears came up through a hole and changed into a spider. Cast Spike Growth — it exploded from the spikes, turned into a burst of worms, spiders, etc. Jingle Jangle did not wake from the noise.
- Called it for the night there.
