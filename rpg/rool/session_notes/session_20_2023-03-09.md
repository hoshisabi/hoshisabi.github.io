### Terrace Town & Saviors of Pixie Town

- Made it back to the Getaway Gang. **Mugan = Will**.
- Gave candy to the kids — had different effects on each: one kid foamed, one kid grew, Star had musical hiccups. Calmer kid.
- Zone of Truth: questioned someone who had been working for Skabitha and trafficking kids. He was either cursed by Endelyn or had a genuine change of heart.
- His plan: **Bela** the pixie would free and protect the children; Will would infiltrate the market and make his presence known to get an audience.
- To get attention and appear valuable, they would prepare the most prized fertilizer known: **10 lbs each of Jabberwock dung**.
- Approached an owlbear that was distressed — calmed her with animal handling (rolled 27). Her owner had been carried off by a big flying creature. Her harness had a name tag: **"Juniper."**
- Went to **Terrace Town**. Met Mayor **Lobana Mossypants**. Offered candy, which had various minor magical effects.
- The town has been feeling disconnected since the mists passed. They were looking for **Tablerise** — searching for a way to reach the floating isles, which they cannot do the way the faerie folk can.
- A goblin poked his head in — his daughter had been taken by Skabitha.
- A contingent of tin soldiers and animated [creatures] were marching about the town.
- Fought the tin soldiers. As they died, some exploded and caught buildings on fire — the party also fought the fires.
- Got a packet of pixie dust for saving the buildings and earned a **Story Award: "Saviors of Pixie Town"** (advantage on Charisma checks with the goblin town).
- *Ended it here.*
