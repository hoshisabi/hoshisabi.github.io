* Started with the prison escape
* Got a book — *Tales of the Gloaming Court* — Glister suggested kissing the frog, which made it say the name of the book in Elvish
* Fought Endelyn and managed to kill her without creating an eclipse
  * She disappeared in smoke — probably not truly dead
* Reunited with the NPCs; Amidor was willing to take us
  * Discussed going upstairs to the belfry to ring the bell and maybe teleport to the palace
* Returned inside; saw the ornithopter; discussed riding it up to the belfry — but it can only take one
* When we rang the bell in the belfry, a flock of giant cranes appeared — one for each of us
* Flew to the **Palace of Heart's Desire**
* *"The lion or the hart must wear the crown, but which one should it weigh down?"* — engraved on the gate
* Inside the gate was a pavilion with noises — a party:
  * A talking brown bear, a human carrying a pumpkin, an elf, a dwarf missing his head, and another human noble
  * Frozen: a halfling and a female orc
  * All arguing about whether clotted cream should be applied to a scone before or after jam
* Picked and ate a golden apple; carried one — gave a bonus to wisdom saving throws
* The water in the fountain didn't move, felt doughy; resumed moving when taken out
* Rose garden had ettercaps that attacked the familiar
  * A satyr in the middle with a vine of roses — black, blue, green, red, and white
* Went to the pond; Acesis caught a firefly; touched the water — a humanoid figure made of black fish held up a silver crown
  * Picked it up — made of needles
  * Heard a voice: *"The beginning of whenever / The end of ever after / The start of an age / The finale of every moment / The first in history"*
  * Answer probably *"wrath"*
* Went into one tower — saw a lion; his name was Envy. Did not give him the crown.
* Climbed the other wall; found an iron hart named **Wrath**
  * Put the iron crown on Wrath; the crown turned into golden roses
* Walked through the door with a hart symbol
  * Stairs going up; towers with lightning
  * Right-hand stairs: white coffer on a black basalt pedestal
* Walked to the left side door
  * Short staircase to another tower
  * Black enameled coffer on a pedestal of living vines — a plum-sized jewel, crystallized desire for Zybilna to know what her enemies are planning
* Went back to the first coffer — a white plum-sized gem
  * Acesis determined it was the desire to show kindness to the destitute
* Crystal carriage hitched to glass pegasi, with silk butterfly wings on the roof
* Each door in the long room had an alabaster pedestal with a vase of white roses — *"Take one"* carved in Common
  * The rug attacked as I crossed to get a rose
* Green coffer with a green crystal on the staircase up
* **Strongheart** the paladin floating in midair, frozen in time
* Found a changing room; found a pool
* Found the ballroom with the **Jabberwock** — stealthed in but woke it
  * Distracted it and ran while others tried to free things
  * Got clipped by the jabberwock — nearly died (4hp remaining)
  * They managed to free **Ringlerun**, whose Cone of Cold went off
  * Managed to escape to the pavilion; freed **Mercion**

*Finished trying to find the true name of Zybilna.*
