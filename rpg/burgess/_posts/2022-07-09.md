* Moved to the next realm; came upon a cave and a tree with parchment nailed to it — a wanted poster for "sorry wretch," reward ten years of your youth
* Went to the cave; saw a man spinning gold while blindfolded; directed us towards Granny Nightshade
* Met a campestri dipping his cap into a pan of boiling butter, waiting for his friend to have dinner together *(Carpenter and Walrus?)*
* Goblin returned; explained there was a candy merchant in Loomlurch
* Acesis made food and water to bargain with the goblin to not eat the campestri
* Took a long rest by the goblin's campfire
* Travelled further; as we got closer to a lake, the path would prevent us from moving closer
* Found another poster: *"This rapscallion vexes me. Deliver him to me alive and unharmed, and I'll curse one creature whom you name."*
* Encountered a dryad and animated shrubs; they attacked; talked her into stopping
  * She thought we were mercenaries from the hag, after the toyshop, or to catch Will
  * Provided 3 sycamore seeds that if eaten will be a pass without trace
* Made our way to Loomlurch; went to the goblin candy merchant
* Talked to Skabitha — she did not want to give up the painting, but agreed to let us see them if we retrieved a unicorn horn
  * Would give the three inches she took from Kizz if we get something for her from her sister
* Went inside the painting at night; discovered by Sowpig; lied and said we were getting paintings cleaned
* Got my finger pricked by a thorn; painting looked like me and my relatives
  * Cut a piece of thorn; grabbed other paintings
  * Idea: Bavlorna can prick her finger and one will be Skabitha
  * Returned paintings to Bavlorna
* She was happy, but said her sister will be furious
  * Had me prick my finger to test the magic; then pricked her own — it worked
