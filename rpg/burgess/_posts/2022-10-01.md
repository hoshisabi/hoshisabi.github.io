* Entered Yon with Squirt's help
* Ran into an anthropomorphized dandelion, a bee the size of an orange, and an elf who drew weapons
  * Dandelion said "She's mine" — helping her escape to return to her sister
  * Dandelion is **Amidor**; bee is **Polinella**; the elf is **Gleam**
* Several warring factions:
  * To the east: briganocks, who are miners
  * To the west: korreds, known for their wiry hair
  * An elf prince who came to visit from his land, near a big lake to the north
* Heading north towards the elf; ran into goblins known as the Dead Ringers
  * Traded scatterleaf tea for 3 questions
    * *"How do we rescue the elf's sister from the hag?"* — "Play to her passions. Stay on script. A cat, a horn, or a shadow ripped."
    * *"How do we stop the creatures harassing the elf?"* — "The creatures of the air were transformed players from afar. Play to their passions and they may desist."
    * *"About the creatures with the wiry hair?"* — "Those of stony hair are the most truthful all around. Stick with them and they will help you for what to be found."
* First night: a fire lit atop a pillar, but nothing else; travelled another day and reached a lake
* Eight columns of rock around the lake; eight winged beats circle a figure in a boat; he went to the top of a pillar, lit a flame — the winged beasts put it out when he left
* Told the elf we meant no harm; he rowed towards us
  * **Alagarthus**, from the Forest of Cormanthor
  * Unable to fend off a green dragon; a hag's shadow coaxed him to this realm
  * Endelyn showed him a future where he survived the dragon, but the deal didn't include a path home
  * The lake is a portal; to activate it he needs to light all eight beacons
  * The peryton were members of a group of performers, the ___ Mummers
  * Put on a play for the perytons, who allowed the elf to light a beacon
  * Lake opened after 8 hours; let him return to Cormanthor
* After leaving, followed by shadowy figures that look like goblins
  * Stared at them, waited, and they eventually "joined us"
* Came upon a small girl flying a kite with a horrible face
  * She cannot stop flying the kite or a curse triggers
  * Curse comes from the five blue bows on the string
  * The kite is blown about by the wind, connected to the girl's mood
  * The two shadows that followed us were her parents' shadows; Endelyn removed them as punishment
  * Talked to the girl, convinced her we would help; wind died down; untied the bow from the kite
* Came upon a game board and a dying fire
  * Made the one move to win the game
  * Offered a stone of good luck and a star sapphire as gifts
  * **Argantle of the Shale** and **Jagu of the Chalk** — went into hiding when we arrived
  * In a feud with the brigganocks, who help Creeping Lyn build wicked contraptions
  * Danced the night away with the korreds
* Went towards the brigand camp
  * Swept up in a tornado; lost a dagger; ended up on top of a beacon
  * Got to the mine; statues outside; sounds of hundreds of pickaxes
  * Fell asleep in the mine; woke up tied down like Gulliver
  * Human in their midst named **Molliver**
  * They thought the korreds worked for Creeping Lyn as well
  * Convinced Molliver the korreds weren't working with her either
  * He had us untied and escorted us through an illusionary wall
* Came to a bridge with three flaming skulls; attacked until we rushed past
* Into a grotto — a piebald pony with a hut on its back, laughter from inside
  * A tiny old brigganock named **Keg** welcomed us
  * They make the wish stones; two of our wishes are here
  * A brigganock with a wheelbarrow brought out two glowing stones
