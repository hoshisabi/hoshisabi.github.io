# Session 1 (May 13, 2026)

*PS-DC-PUB-08 — Unremembered Things*

They came to in a tavern that smelled like spilled liquor and copper. Bodies were arranged around them — not piled, but arranged — and everyone was holding something: a dagger, a holy symbol, a pitchfork. Somewhere in the last few hours, something had gone very wrong, and none of them could remember what.

A stranger in a corner booth was asking about his sister.

Sergio Bloodstone, private investigator, had the same problem they did — no memory, but slightly more context. He'd been working a case: a dangerous book stolen from the Hall of Records, a client who insisted it be kept shut. He thought they were all probably working for him. The pay would be two hundred gold each. Nobody argued with the rate, given the bodies.

What followed was an investigation threading through Pandemonium's impossible geography — the House of Late Blessings, Burton's Building Supplies, the Asylum. The clues cohered slowly: a cache of bottles containing a liquor that stripped memory like paint, six-fingered tattoos pointing to Grazit's followers, a woman named Marla Swift Strike whose role in all of it turned out to be more than it appeared. A notebook. A journal. A trail leading from the Bloodstone syndicate's criminal network to whatever was behind the stolen book and the memory-erasing drink.

The climax came in a haunted office, ascending floor by floor to a confrontation with something that should not have been there. They came out with vital documents and a resolution: the liquor burns, the book is destroyed. The shadowy mastermind behind all of it is still out there.

For a group with no memory, they found out quite a lot.
