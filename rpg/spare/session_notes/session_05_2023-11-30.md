### Chardalyn Madness & Sled Dogs

- Found a desk with dark crystals on it.
- A burlap sack covered an object emitting light, which turned out to be the stolen magical lantern.
- A dwarf in the middle of the room blew up.
- Discovered a letter containing information about chardalyn.
- Identified the black crystal as chardalyn, which is suffused with demonic magic capable of causing madness.
- Found that the bodies in the area had chardalyn growing directly inside them.
- Moved to another room and broke down another door.
- Found cells holding fungal/humanoid creatures that attacked when we tried to pass them.
- Skorn killed one of the creatures (possibly named Picket).
- Recovered a sack of pearls.
- Returned the stolen lantern to the town; in exchange, we received free room and board, returned our borrowed gear, and got drinks.
- Tanis Syve's expedition returned the pearls.
- Met Jarthra, whose associate is facing the merchant guilds.
- Jarthra offered us his sled and the 6 sled dogs that pull it.
- Examined a corpse and confirmed chardalyn is growing directly out of the bone.
- Received *Bracers of Archery* from the Speaker.
- Dropped the chardalyn-infected body off at the mines with my patron (kept the details quiet).
- Broke there.
