### Caer-Konig & The Duergar Outpost

- Travelled to Caer-Konig, a journey of about 12.5 hours through the cold.
- Checked in at the local tavern, the "Hook, Line, and Sinker".
- Met the Speaker of Caer-Konig, a dragonborn named Trovis, who was extremely drunk.
- Trovis rambled about his nightly "patrols" and mentioned that a magical lantern and several other miscellaneous items had been stolen from the town.
- Discovered that the thefts were connected to suspicious tracks leading toward Kelvin's Cairn.
- Followed the tracks up the snowy slopes of Kelvin's Cairn.
- Discovered a hidden stone fortress, which served as an outpost for the duergar (dark dwarves).
- Confronted the duergar guards at the drawbridge. They released a zombie ogre to attack us.
- Defeated the bridge guards and the zombie ogre, though the alarm was raised.
- Entered the outpost and found a room containing two caged goats.
- Explored further and triggered a corridor trap; wall-mounted spikes impaled Winnie and Skorn.
- Skorn destroyed the spikes and found a lever that retracted the remaining trap mechanisms.
- Skorn broke down a heavy wooden door to progress deeper into the fortress.
- Initiated combat with the duergar waiting in the room beyond the door. Left off mid-combat.
