### Duergar Tracks & The White Moose

- Returned to Bryn Shander (a multi-hour journey, but we avoided fatigue).
- Visited the library and presented the chardalyn-infected body to Velene, the kobold guard/researcher.
- Velene cast *Gentle Repose* on the body, and we obtained a scroll of *Gentle Repose* from her.
- Conducted research in the library while Velene was occupied. Scribed *Gentle Repose* into Spare's spellbook.
- Verified that duergar are relatively new arrivals to this region and noted the correct precautions for handling chardalyn.
- Visited the Sheriff's office to report our findings. Learned about missing siblings in Dougan's Hole.
- Travelled to Termalaine where Skorn entered a pit fight against a displaced miner. The mine had been closed due to monsters rising from the depths. They ended up playing friendly games with one another.
- Met the Captain of the Easthaven Militia and headed toward the Easthaven Ferry.
- Snuck in, but Winnie was attacked by duergar who praised Xardorok Sunblight as they fought us.
- One of the duergar shrunk in size during the fight and remained shrunk after dying.
- Recovered the bodies of both the shrunk duergar and a normal duergar adorned with chardalyn spikes.
- Noticed a single, anomalous footprint leading away from the ferry as we went to leave.
- Followed the tracks and discovered that the escapee had mounted an axebeak.
- Learned that some hunters are being attacked in Lonelywood, and heard warnings about the dreaded White Moose.
- In Termalaine, the Speaker has officially cleared out the mine.
- In Good Mead, the Speaker was killed by a marauder during a mead theft, and several town guards are missing.
- Noted a local Shrine of Tempus used for worship.
- Discovered the grizzly remains of 5 militia members who had been clubbed to death.
- Skorn released a trapped fox, which began following him. Skorn named the fox Todd.
- Discovered a miniature chwinga. It vanished back into the woods (failed a Constitution save and suffered 1 level of exhaustion).
- Skorn received a *Blessing of Animal Conjuring*. The chwinga and Todd eventually departed.
- Left off at the entrance to a cave.
