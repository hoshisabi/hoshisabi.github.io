### Riot in Bryn Shander & The Last Session

- Finished off the mammoth that had been knocked unconscious.
- Located the missing children, who were actually married relatives. They were severely frostbitten.
- Escorted them safely back to Dougan's Hole. In gratitude, we were rewarded with *Boots of the Winterlands*.
- Traveled to Good Mead and rested.
- Returned to Bryn Shander and walked directly into a riot.
- During the chaos, Copper (the rock gnome) was killed, and a menacing note was slipped into Mishann's pocket.
- The authorities/rioters are forcing an increase in the town sacrifices/offerings to Auril the Frostmaiden.
- In protest and self-defense, the church leaders are planning to close the temple.
- Visited the local blacksmith and outfitter to restock.
- Attempted to seek assistance from Velene but were unable to obtain her help.
- Set off toward Termalaine.
