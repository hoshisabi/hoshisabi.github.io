### Cold Killer & Lake Monster

- Met with the Sheriff of Bryn Shander who requested our help.
- A local merchant named Torrga Icevein runs a traveling caravan/shop called Torg's.
- Torrga has a bodyguard named Sephek Kaltro who is suspected of murdering people who avoided the town lotteries (sacrifices to Auril the Frostmaiden).
- Left Bryn Shander and traveled north to Targos.
- Found out the caravan had already moved on but was currently in Targos.
- Met the Speaker of Targos. In Targos, the lottery sacrifices are for food.
- Heard Torrga's caravan was heading to Bremen next.
- Decided to travel to Bremen (about a 2-hour walk).
- Arrived in Bremen and visited the local tavern.
- Met a dwarf researcher named Hengar who is studying a rumored giant lake monster in Maer Dualdon. Hengar offered to pay if we investigate it.
- Went out onto the lake in a boat loaned by Hengar.
- Encountered a giant plesiosaurus in the lake.
- Discovered the plesiosaurus can speak Common. It explained it was given intelligence by a druid named Ravisin, who wants to punish the towns for their greed and disrespect of nature.
- Successfully persuaded the plesiosaurus to stop attacking boats.
- Returned to town. Hengar rewarded us with 5 gp each and a *Potion of Water Breathing*.
- Spent the night in Bremen.
- The next morning, we saw Torrga's caravan arrive in Bremen.
- Confronted Sephek Kaltro about the murders.
- Sephek was cold and indifferent, claiming he was merely doing Auril's work.
- Fought and defeated Sephek Kaltro, who utilized ice magic and possessed rapid regeneration.
- Found a note on Sephek's body signed by "R." (Ravisin).
- Torrga was extremely unhappy about losing her bodyguard but did not attempt to fight us.
