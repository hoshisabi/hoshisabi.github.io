### Night of Offerings & The Red Wizard

*Party: Skorn (Anthony — large burly half-orc with scars), Darthal (Alex — covered in furs, well-kept 20s), Winnie (Sarah — halfling, neat pigtails), Tamwyn (Kate), Brandon (Goliath — tall, lived outside society, one of the best hunters), Spare.*

- Started in Lonelywood. Tonight is the night of offerings.
- The party received a letter asking them to come to Bryn Shander for Sheriff Southwell.
- In town, locals offer "warmth" by putting out fires.
- Spare is operating out of the mines — working as eyes and ears for a woman in the study (human, her 60s), who sends him to report to Sheriff Southwell. Also mentioned: Father Lake (priest of Auril — owl mask, six-pointed star) and Velene (mines).
- Sheriff Southwell calls the party in to be made deputies. Three murders to investigate. Suspect: Seffick (also written Sephic), connected to the merchant group Torg's, owned by Torgus. Victims are individuals who may have cheated the town lottery — Torgus looks comfortable in cold weather he shouldn't be comfortable in.
- Given access to the Quartermaster: all non-magical goods and services at 50% discount.
- Ordered route: Bryn Shander → Easthaven → Targos.
- Shopped and prepped to set out.
- Encountered ghouls eating a corpse. The body was Daedric Bucklehse — killed by someone with a razor-sharp blade of ice. Not natural ice; radiates evocation magic. Kept in a cloth container.
- Made their way to Easthaven. Saw Torgus arriving in armor, not wearing a jacket.
- A man in red robes was about to be publicly sacrificed. Amara identified him as a Red Wizard of Thay.
- Alex and Anthony stayed behind to deal with Seffick while the others went for help.
- They talked to / intimidated Seffick, who let on that he knew the party were deputies and that he was there to gather information. Sent him away. Seffick had glowing blue eyes.
- A crowd had gathered to kill the Red Wizard using torches to ignite the wood.
- Met Indra Arlegesh, Captain of Easthaven. The Red Wizard had killed the previous party that had been helping them.
- Captain wants to meet at midnight.
- Seffick departed; headed to the White Lady's Inn.
