### The Freycloak Estate & Harpies at the Cave

- Resumed at the town hall. Captain called for help.
- Task: recover the "Ashmen" — people who have gone missing (not only ogres are among the missing).
- A boat called the *Bunch of Knuckleheads* is involved.
- NPC available to sell: Locate Person or Locate Object.
- Tamwyn had to give up the name they had found out about: Ezimir Freycloak, a fur trader who helps make clothing and cloaks.
- The Captain escorted the party to Ezimir's estate quickly.
- Arrived to find the guards killed. No evidence of ice in the wounds.
- Started collecting evidence. The others heard glass smashing.
- Chased down Sephek and found him before he could kill Ezimir.
- Fought. Barely won. Brought back his body and a note. Received a reward: *Scroll of Fireball*.
- A bard at the White Lady's Inn told them the legend of the White Lady herself — her husband's treasure lies at the bottom of the lake.
- Searched for a missing fisherman. Found a missing rowboat near 80-foot cliffs. Caves dot the cliff face; the wind tearing through them sounds like moaning.
- Searched the empty rowboat: fishing gear and empty bottles inside. Footprints lead toward the cave.
- Found harpies on the path to the cave. Fought them. Continued on to the cave entrance.
- Ended session here.
