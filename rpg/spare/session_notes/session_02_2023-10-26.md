### The Missing Iron & Goblin Wagon

- Returned to Bryn Shander.
- Met with Sheriff Markham Southwell to report Sephek Kaltro's death. Received the 100 gp bounty.
- Sheriff Southwell gave us a new task: a caravan carrying a shipment of iron went missing on the way to Bryn Shander.
- Visited Mishann, the priestess of the House of the Morning (shrine of Lathander). She was friendly and helpful, providing us some local guidance.
- Left Bryn Shander to search for the missing caravan, traveling east toward Caer-Dineval.
- Arrived at Caer-Dineval. The local castle was closed and barred to the public, but the town itself was open.
- Gathered information from local townspeople and discovered the caravan was likely ambushed by goblins.
- Tracked the footprints and wagon wheels out into the snowy tundra.
- Located the goblin wagon, which was a large sled pulled by two polar bears.
- The goblins were led by a goblin named Izobel.
- Engaged in combat with the goblins, successfully defeating them and securing the wagon.
- Discovered the polar bears pulling the sled were peaceful and cooperative once freed from their goblin handlers.
- Escorted the sled and the recovered iron shipment back to Bryn Shander.
- Returned the iron to the merchant guild and was rewarded with another 100 gp bounty.
