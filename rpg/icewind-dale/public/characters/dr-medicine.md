# Doctor Medicine

**Player:** Henry
**Race:** Human
**Class:** Warlock 4 (Archfey Patron)
**Background:** Charlatan
**D&D Beyond:** [Character Sheet](https://www.dndbeyond.com/characters/164677217)

![Doctor Medicine](/rpg/icewind-dale/public/characters/images/dr-medicine.png)

## Description

A human warlock whose Archfey patron contacts him through letters that appear in his book. Charisma 18. Background: Charlatan. The name alone tells you something. Speaks Common, Draconic, and Elvish. Carries fine clothes, a costume, a Forgery Kit, Alchemist's Supplies, parchment, and ink — someone who is always prepared to be whoever the situation requires. His Healer feat means "Doctor" is at least partially functional.

## Personality

Fully committed to the bit at all times. Sells snake oil to anything that will hold still long enough. Has conned baby beholders, a muddy spring guardian, and at least one orc fighter (the "speed potion" incident remains technically unresolved). The charlatan persona and the warlock powers are deployed with equal confidence, and it is not always clear which one is doing more work. The Archfey patron's notes apparently tell him where to be, which he finds both useful and annoying.
