# Session 1 — April 25, 2026

The party picked up mid-combat, dispatching the last wolf from an ambush that had already devastated the caravan. What followed was a grinding survival session across the frozen wastes of Icewind Dale — no dungeon, no villain, just weather trying to kill everyone. They scavenged the wreckage for shelter materials, cracked open a lockbox to find someone named Jordan's mother's chocolate chip cookies and a heartfelt letter, inadvertently dyed themselves rainbow colors from a shattered potion vial, and braved a quicksnow field with their guide Pasha before razor-sharp sheets of ice sent them sprinting for cover. The supernatural element arrived with glowing yellow eyes in the blizzard and a flight of four giant owls — elemental manifestations, not natural beasts, conjured by something with an agenda. River went down in the first attack, Dr. Medicine patched her up with exactly the kind of field surgery you'd expect from someone named Doctor Medicine, and the party and their sidekicks ground out the fight. After finding a frozen caravan member clutching a Potion of Healing and a platinum sapphire ring, they located a warm cave and settled in for a long rest. They woke to find their expert sidekick torn limb from limb, the pieces arranged in a geometric pattern, with the tracks of a large creature already half-erased by the snow. Pasha told them it had their scent — it would come back every night. The party voted to hunt it immediately. They followed Pasha into the blizzard, lost her in the whiteout, traced their way back to the cave, and found no sign of her. Session ended with two sidekicks gone and something out there that knows what they smell like.
