# Coldpeak Camp

The home of the Coldpeak, an orc tribe currently under siege from Frostclaw and whatever is driving her. The party arrived here at the end of Session 2, vouched for by Savin and Pasha, and has been guests since.

The tribe relies on the mountain goat flocks for dairy — not just meat. Frostclaw attacking those flocks is an existential threat, not just a danger to hunters. And the hunters have been going missing.

Broken Tusk leads. Savin is being groomed to succeed her against his will. Kaarsk runs the hunts. The tribe has known about the Netherese ruins in the mountain for generations and has always avoided them. Two orc guards were found dead outside the camp entrance after Session 3, their bodies arranged around a rune of goat entrails with a single bloody white feather at the center. The rune read: *Soon.*
