# Thunlakalaga Camp

The home of the Thunlakalaga, a Goliath tribe currently under siege from the Owlbear and whatever is driving it. The party arrived here at the end of Session 2, vouched for by Sook and Pasha, and has been guests since.

The tribe relies on the mountain goat flocks for dairy — not just meat. The Owlbear attacking those flocks is an existential threat, not just a danger to hunters. And the hunters have been going missing.

Old Goat leads. Sook is being groomed to succeed her against his will. Kaskur runs the hunts. The tribe knows about the Netherese ruins in the mountain and has always avoided them. Two Goliath guards were found dead outside the camp entrance after Session 3, their bodies arranged around a rune of goat entrails with a single bloody white feather at the center. The rune read: *Soon.*
