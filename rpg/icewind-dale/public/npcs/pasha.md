# Pasha

**Role:** Warrior / guide
**Status:** Active — returned in Session 2

![Pasha](/rpg/icewind-dale/wiki/characters/npcs/images/pasha.png)

Experienced wilderness warrior and guide who accompanied the party from the caravan. Survived the avalanche and wolf attack that destroyed most of it. Led the party through quicksnow, sheltered them from razor snow, and was the one who identified the creature that had developed their scent and would hunt them every night until dealt with.

After the expert sidekick's remains were found arranged in a geometric pattern, Pasha insisted on hunting the creature immediately — no waiting. The party followed her into the blizzard. They lost sight of her in the snow. When they retraced their steps she was gone.

She turned up alive in Session 2, having spent the intervening time in the wilderness before finding Sook and making her way to the Thunlakalaga camp. She had interacted with Goliath tribes before, though not this one specifically, and helped guide the party through some of the tribe's customs. She has not said much about what happened during the time she was missing.
