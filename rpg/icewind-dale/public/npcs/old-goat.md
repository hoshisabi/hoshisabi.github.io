# Old Goat

**Role:** Chieftain, Thunlakalaga tribe
**Race:** Goliath
**Status:** Active

![Old Goat](/rpg/icewind-dale/wiki/characters/npcs/images/old-goat.png)

Elder chieftain of the Thunlakalaga. Has been watching people lie about the Netherese ruins in the mountain for thirty years. Smokes a pipe. Her nickname is Old Goat; she has heard every joke about it.

She is the one who suggested bringing the party along when Kaskur's hunters went missing — not because she trusts outsiders, but because she recognized that people who survived an Owlbear encounter and walked out of those ruins might be useful. She receives information and decides what to do with it. The tribe's response to the current crisis runs through her.

*The party has not yet met her directly.*
