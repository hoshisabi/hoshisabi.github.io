Doctor Medicine's fey spirit. Small, stocky, and unsettling in the way only something ancient and gleeful about hurting people can be. Wears his cap red for a reason. The iron-shod boots are not decorative.

He arrives when called, does what is asked, and goes back wherever redcaps go between summons. Whether he enjoys the work is not in question.

First called in Session 9 to deal with the chardalyn warriors charging the crowd. Fey Step, 10 force damage, problem acknowledged.

The Archfey patron's letters apparently mentioned him by name before Doctor Medicine knew to ask.
