Two Coldpeak sentries. Killed by Rimetalon during the night following the party's arrival at camp. Their bodies were arranged — deliberately — in a rune of goat entrails around a single white feather. The rune said *Soon*.

Their spears were planted in the ground at the east perimeter before sunrise, pointed outward toward what killed them. No ceremony. No speech. That is how it is done.

Kaarsk told Berg that the spirits of the fallen won't rest until their killer is confronted. Not defeated — confronted. The attempt is what matters. They have one week.

*Their names have not yet been spoken to the party.*
