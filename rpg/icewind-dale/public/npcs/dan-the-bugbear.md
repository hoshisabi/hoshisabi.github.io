One of the sidekicks who accompanied the party from the beginning of the campaign. Present during Session 1 — the caravan wreckage, the elemental owl fight, and the night in the warm cave.

He was killed by Rimetalon — the party didn't have a name for it yet. His intestines were used to spell out a symbol of unknown meaning.
