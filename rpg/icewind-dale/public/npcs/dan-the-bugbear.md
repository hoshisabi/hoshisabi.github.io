One of the sidekicks who accompanied the party from the beginning of the campaign. A former circus performer who crossed paths with Dr. Medicine somewhere in his travels — the details of how a bugbear ends up doing tricks for a traveling show are not entirely clear, but his patchwork costume suggested a career that predated the tundra by some margin.

Present during Session 1 — the caravan wreckage, the elemental owl fight, and the night in the warm cave.

He was killed by Rimetalon — the party didn't have a name for it yet. His intestines were used to spell out a symbol of unknown meaning.
