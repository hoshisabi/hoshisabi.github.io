# Kaskur

**Role:** Lead hunter, Thunlakalaga tribe
**Race:** Goliath
**Status:** Active

![Kaskur](/rpg/icewind-dale/wiki/characters/npcs/images/kaskur.png)

The Thunlakalaga's lead hunter. Tall even by Goliath standards, carries a massive longbow across his chest with hatchets and a knife for close work. Skeptical of outsiders and does not hide it. When Old Goat suggested bringing the party along to find the missing hunters, Kaskur agreed — barely — and immediately made clear the terms: *don't slow us down*.

He moved at a pace that required an Athletics check just to keep up. When a hunter fell through the ice and took exhaustion, Kaskur sent him home with an escort and kept going without breaking stride. After the wolf fight he skinned the pack himself, and gave River a nod for helping — which is apparently as close to a compliment as he gets.

Berg speaking Giant on the first day made an impression. Kaskur has been quietly revising his read of the party ever since, though he hasn't said so.
