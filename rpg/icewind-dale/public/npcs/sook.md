# Sook

**Role:** Hunter, Thunlakalaga tribe
**Race:** Goliath
**Status:** Active ally

![Sook](/rpg/icewind-dale/wiki/characters/npcs/images/sook.png)

A young Goliath hunter from the Thunlakalaga (they/them). Found Pasha wandering in the blizzard after she separated from the party and escorted both her and the party to the tribe's camp. Vouched for the outsiders to Old Goat on the basis that they had survived an Owlbear encounter.

Was being quietly groomed for tribal leadership by Old Goat. Does not want it. The tribe is in crisis, which is not making that situation easier.

Has a scar on their right hand that they do not discuss.
