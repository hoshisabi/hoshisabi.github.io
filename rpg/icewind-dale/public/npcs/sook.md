A young hunter from the Coldpeak tribe. Half-orc — his father, a Reghedsman, came to live with the tribe and married Broken Tusk's sister, who died shortly after Savin was born. He has grown up moving between both families, never entirely belonging to either world. Has a scar on his right hand that he deflects questions about.

Found Pasha wandering in the blizzard after she separated from the party, and escorted both her and the party to the tribe's camp. Vouched for the outsiders to Broken Tusk on the basis that they had survived Frostclaw.

Was being quietly groomed for tribal leadership by Broken Tusk. Does not want it. The tribe is in crisis, which is not making that situation easier. Kaarsk has never hidden his contempt.
