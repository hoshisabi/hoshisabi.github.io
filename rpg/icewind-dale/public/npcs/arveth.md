Not the mountain. The mountain is where her reach touches the world from inside the Dale — and from inside the Dale, there is no difference that matters.

The Coldpeak call the mountain what everyone calls it: *the mountain over there*, spoken with deliberate care, without naming it. You do not name a thing you fear. The party inherited that name without quite knowing they were inheriting anything.

She communicates through images: stone, depth, patience, a thing held shut. [Clod](clod) serves as her Harbinger, translating her images into language; his translations are confident and imprecise in ways that reflect his own bias. He came to the role through a brief guardianship of his own — the full account is in [Session 4](../sessions/2026-05-15).

The terms are settled. [Doctor Medicine](../characters/dr-medicine) carries a pebble — white striations in it, taken from the chamber walls — so that Arveth can see through it always. In exchange, she granted him one small change in the past. He accepted her as patron, committing to Great Old One before the session was out. Clod announced the arrangement with as much solemnity as he could muster. ([Session 12](../sessions/2026-07-17))

*Nothing will ever have been — and it didn't have to.*

## Roleplaying notes

Arveth communicates through images to anyone who is awake and present: stone, depth, web, a thread plucked and vibrating through the whole chain. No words. When she speaks directly — bypassing images entirely, making contact — the recipient goes still. Comatose. The vision comes from inside. To the rest of the room they just stop. What the recipient experiences is something vast and loud, even at a whisper.

She does not experience urgency as a category. The pace of what she offers is the pace of mountains.
