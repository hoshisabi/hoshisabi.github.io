Junior field researcher for the Arcane Brotherhood, out of Luskan. She found Renier's shelter the same morning the party did — a convergence she will spend several professional reports trying to classify correctly.

She was possessed by Yael the entire time the party was in the shelter, which she does not remember. What she does remember: arriving, taking notes, the moment she woke on the floor with a stabilization wound in her chest and five adventurers standing around her. She handled the gap with the professionalism of someone who has learned to treat field research as managing what is actually knowable. Kept her composure. Asked the questions an honest researcher asks when several days of her field log are blank.

She took Renier's journal for safekeeping — the right call for preservation; not necessarily the right call for the party. She left them a token of invitation to the Brotherhood's regional outpost.

She owes the party her life. She does not know this. She does know they were there when she woke up, and that one of them stabilized her mid-combat while citing what he called the hypocritical oath. This has made an impression.

She is junior enough to be recruited, ignored, or manipulated. A senior name from Luskan has already taken an interest in her field report. She doesn't know that either.
