# Kaskur

**Race:** Goliath
**Role:** Lead hunter, Thunlakalaga tribe
**Status:** Active — accompanied party in Session 3

## Description

The tribe's lead hunter. Tall even by Goliath standards, carries a massive longbow across his chest along with hatchets and a knife for close work. Deeply skeptical of outsiders and makes no effort to hide it. Does not waste words. When Berg spoke Giant and declared they would slay the beast, Kaskur repeated it — not as agreement, but as a statement of what was required.

## Known History

Grumbled about taking the party along to find the missing hunters. Agreed reluctantly after Old Goat's suggestion and Dr. Medicine's note-from-the-boot persuasion. Led the party at a brutal pace across the frozen wilderness. When a hunter fell through the ice and took exhaustion, Kaskur sent him back with an escort without hesitation and continued on with just the party. Helped skin the wolves after the fight and gave River a nod of approval for helping.

## Relationships

- Thunlakalaga tribe; reports to Old Goat
- Wary respect developing toward Berg (fellow warrior, speaks Giant)
- Tolerates the rest of the party

## Session Notes

- Session 3: Led party to find missing hunters; survived the wolf pack encounter; skinned the wolves
