# Old Goat

**Race:** Goliath
**Role:** Chieftain, Thunlakalaga tribe
**Status:** Not yet met

## Description

Elder chieftain of the Thunlakalaga tribe. Described as someone who has been watching people lie about the Netherese ruins for thirty years. Wants to meet the party after hearing they survived the Owlbear encounter.

## Known History

Has led the Thunlakalaga through the current crisis. The tribe has apparently known about the Netherese chamber for generations and has always avoided it. The Owlbear's recent behavior has changed that calculation.
