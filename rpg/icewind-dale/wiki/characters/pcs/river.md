# Roaring River

**Player:** Eric
**Race:** Tabaxi
**Class:** Rogue 3 (Swashbuckler)
**Background:** Mulhorandi Tomb Raider
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
**D&D Beyond:** https://www.dndbeyond.com/characters/164761052

## Description

A Tabaxi with a climbing speed equal to walking (30 ft.), Darkvision 60 ft., and Cat's Claws for unarmed strikes. Speaks Common, Dwarvish, Sylvan, and Thieves' Cant — an unusual language spread that hints at a varied history. Background as a Mulhorandi Tomb Raider suggests experience with traps, ancient places, and things best left undisturbed.

## Combat

Mobile and opportunistic. Swashbuckler subclass means:
- **Rakish Audacity** — can Sneak Attack without an adjacent ally as long as no other enemies are nearby, and gets CHA to initiative
- **Fancy Footwork** — melee attacks don't trigger opportunity attacks
- **Feline Agility** — can double speed once per turn (Tabaxi trait)

Feat: **Lucky** (2 Luck Points/long rest) — can grant self advantage or impose disadvantage on an incoming attack roll.

Expertise in Acrobatics and Stealth (+5 and +7 respectively). Strong Perception (Passive 15). Weapons lean finesse: dual rapiers, dagger, shortbow. Cat's Claws as backup unarmed.
