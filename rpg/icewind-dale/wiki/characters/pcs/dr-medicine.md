# Doctor Medicine

**Player:** Henry
**Race:** Human
**Class:** Warlock 4 (Archfey Patron)
**Background:** Charlatan
**D&D Beyond:** https://www.dndbeyond.com/characters/164677217

## Description

A charlatan warlock with an Archfey patron, Charisma 18, a Forgery Kit, and Alchemist's Supplies. The name alone tells you something. Speaks Common, Draconic, and Elvish. Carries fine clothes, a costume, parchment, and ink — someone who is always prepared to be whoever the situation requires.

## Combat

Charisma-first, mobile battlefield disruptor. Key features:
- **Eldritch Blast** — +6 to hit, 1d10+4 Force (Agonizing Blast adds CHA mod)
- **Armor of Shadows** — casts Mage Armor on self at will (no slot), hence AC 15
- **Devil's Sight** — sees normally in magical and nonmagical darkness out to 120 ft.
- **Steps of the Fey** — Misty Step 4x/long rest, with choice of:
  - *Refreshing Step:* teleport grants self or nearby ally 1d10 temp HP
  - *Taunting Step:* creatures left behind have Disadvantage on attacks against others until next turn

Spellcasting: CHA, DC 14, +6 attack. Notable spells:
- *Cantrips:* Eldritch Blast, Prestidigitation, Minor Illusion
- *Archfey always prepared:* Faerie Fire, Sleep, Calm Emotions, Phantasmal Force, Misty Step
- *1st:* Protection from Evil and Good, Witch Bolt, Armor of Agathys, Charm Person
- *2nd (2 Pact slots):* Hold Person, Misty Step

**Feats:** Spell Sniper (ignore half/three-quarters cover, no Disadvantage in melee, +60 ft. range on attack-roll spells), Healer (Battle Medic action to spend Healer's Kit for HP recovery), Skilled.
