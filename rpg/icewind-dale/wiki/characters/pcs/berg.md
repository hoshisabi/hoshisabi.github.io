# Berg Wurdnowwah

**Player:** Josh
**Race:** Orc
**Class:** Fighter 4 (Battle Master)
**Background:** Soldier
**Alignment:** —
**D&D Beyond:** https://www.dndbeyond.com/characters/164719669

## Description

Orc fighter built like a wall — AC 22 in chain mail, 40 HP, Darkvision 180 ft. Speaks Common, Giant, and Orc. Proficient with smith's tools and dragonchess, which says something about the person underneath the armor.

## Combat

Tactical and selfless fighter. Battle Master maneuvers focus on protecting and enabling allies:
- **Distracting Strike** — grants an ally advantage on the next attack
- **Bait and Switch** — swaps position with a willing ally, giving one of them an AC bonus
- **Rally** — bonus action to give a nearby ally temporary HP

Additional tools: Action Surge, Relentless Endurance (drop to 1 HP instead of 0, once/long rest), Adrenaline Rush (bonus action Dash for temp HP). Feats include Interception (reaction to reduce damage to a nearby ally), Charger, Savage Attacker, and Tough.

Primary weapons: Pike (reach, push) and Javelin (thrown, slow). Unarmed strikes at +5.
