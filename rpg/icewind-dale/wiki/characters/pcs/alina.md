# Alina Shandorath

**Player:** Dominic
**Race:** Tiefling (Cthonic Legacy)
**Class:** Wizard 4 (Evoker)
**Background:** Guide
**D&D Beyond:** https://www.dndbeyond.com/characters/164743608

## Description

Tiefling wizard with Necrotic resistance and a cartographer's eye for the world. Speaks Common, Draconic, and Goblin. Carries three costumes, a flute, and a bullseye lantern — someone who moves through the world in many shapes and prefers to see clearly in the dark.

## Combat

Evoker focused on battlefield control and damage. Key features:
- **Evocation Savant** — added Witch Bolt to spellbook for free; gets a free Evocation spell each level
- **Potent Cantrip** — missed cantrips still deal half damage
- **Arcane Recovery** — recovers up to 2 levels of spell slots on a short rest (once/long rest)

Spellcasting: INT, DC 12, +4 attack. Notable spells:
- *Cantrips:* Ray of Frost, Fire Bolt, Chill Touch, Mind Sliver, Acid Splash, Guidance, Spare the Dying, Thaumaturgy
- *1st:* Witch Bolt, Shield, Mage Armor, Magic Missile, Detect Magic, Healing Word, False Life
- *2nd:* Misty Step, Enlarge/Reduce, Cloud of Daggers

**Tiefling traits:** Cthonic Legacy (Necrotic resistance), Otherworldly Presence (Thaumaturgy via Charisma).
**Feat:** Tough (+8 HP base, +2/level thereafter — hence 34 HP despite d6 hit dice). Magic Initiate (Druid) for Guidance, Healing Word, and Spare the Dying.
