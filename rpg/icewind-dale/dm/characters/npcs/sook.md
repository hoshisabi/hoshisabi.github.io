# Savin

**Race:** Half-orc
**Role:** Hunter, Coldpeak tribe
**Status:** Active ally
**Pronouns:** he/him

## Description

Hunter for the Coldpeak tribe. Half-orc — his father is a Reghedsman who came to live with the tribe and married Broken Tusk's sister. His mother died shortly after he was born. He has grown up moving between both families, comfortable in neither world entirely. Has a scar on his right hand that he deflects questions about quickly.

Kaarsk has never hidden his contempt for Savin — he represents everything Kaarsk resents: the peace with the Reghedsmen, the dilution of the tribe's bloodline, the softening of what it means to be Coldpeak. Broken Tusk has been quietly grooming Savin for leadership. Savin does not want it.

## Known History

Found Pasha wandering in the blizzard after she separated from the party and brought her to the tribe. Was astounded the party encountered Frostclaw and survived — vouched for them to Broken Tusk on that basis.
