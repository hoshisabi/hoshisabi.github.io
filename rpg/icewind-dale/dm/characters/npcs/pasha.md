# Pasha

**Role:** Warrior sidekick / guide
**Status:** Active — returned Session 2

## Description

Experienced guide and warrior. Speaks with authority about the dangers of Icewind Dale — knows what razor snow is, recognized the elemental nature of the yellow eyes in the blizzard, and was the one who identified the creature as something that had "developed their scent" and would hunt the party every night until dealt with.

Puts on a brave face even when frightened. Determined to the point of recklessness — insisted on hunting the creature immediately despite blizzard conditions.

## Known History

Accompanied the party from the caravan. Survived the avalanche/wolf attack that destroyed most of the caravan. Led the party through quicksnow and sheltered them from razor snow. After discovering the expert sidekick's remains arranged in a geometric pattern, insisted on hunting the creature rather than waiting.

Led the party into the blizzard to track the creature. The party lost sight of her and were led off course by a trick in the snow. When they retraced their steps, she was gone — she didn't leave them, they left her.

## Session Notes

- Session 1: Separated in blizzard while hunting the creature. Status unknown.
- Session 2: Emerged alive from the wilderness, accompanied by Sook. Found outside the Netherese ruins after the party fought the Owlbear. Had spent the intervening time surviving in the wild before finding Savin and making her way toward the Coldpeak camp. Has dealt with orc tribes before — not this one specifically — and helped guide the party through some of the tribe's customs. Has said nothing about what happened while she was missing.
