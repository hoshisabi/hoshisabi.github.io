# Broken Tusk

**Race:** Orc
**Role:** Chieftain, Coldpeak tribe
**Status:** Active

## Description

Elder chieftain of the Coldpeak. Has been watching people lie about the Netherese ruins in the mountain for thirty years. Smokes a pipe. Her name is Broken Tusk; she has heard every joke about it.

Her sister married a Reghedsman who came to live with the tribe; the sister died shortly after Savin was born. This makes Savin her nephew. The peace she has made with the Reghedsmen is therefore personal, not just political — which is part of why Kaarsk's contempt for it cuts so deep.

## Known History

Has led the Coldpeak through the current crisis. The tribe has known about the Netherese chamber for generations and has always avoided it. Frostclaw's recent behavior has changed that calculation.
