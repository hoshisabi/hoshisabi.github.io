# Kaarsk

**Race:** Orc
**Role:** Lead hunter, Coldpeak tribe
**Status:** Active — accompanied party in Session 3

## Description

The tribe's lead hunter. Carries a massive longbow across his chest along with hatchets and a knife for close work. Deeply skeptical of outsiders and makes no effort to hide it. Does not waste words. When Berg spoke Orcish and declared they would slay the beast, Kaarsk repeated it — not as agreement, but as a statement of what was required.

Resents the Reghedsmen who wander into Coldpeak territory and take game the tribe can't spare. Resents the peace Broken Tusk has made with them. Resents Savin as a daily embodiment of both. Kaarsk is not wrong that times are hard and the hunting grounds are shrinking — he is wrong about what it means.

## Known History

Grumbled about taking the party along to find the missing hunters. Agreed reluctantly after Broken Tusk's suggestion and Dr. Medicine's note-from-the-boot persuasion. Led the party at a brutal pace across the frozen wilderness. When a hunter fell through the ice and took exhaustion, Kaarsk sent him back with an escort without hesitation and continued on with just the party. Helped skin the wolves after the fight and gave River a nod of approval for helping.

## Relationships

- Coldpeak tribe; reports to Broken Tusk
- Wary respect developing toward Berg (fellow warrior, speaks Orcish — an orc among orcs)
- Contempt for Savin; barely tolerates the rest of the party

## Session Notes

- Session 3: Led party to find missing hunters; survived the wolf pack encounter; skinned the wolves
