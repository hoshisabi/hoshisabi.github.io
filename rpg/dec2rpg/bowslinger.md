# Bowslinger

**Bowslinger** [Fighter]  
**Type** : General  
**Source** : Underdark

You can ready ranged weapons surprisingly quickly.

Prerequisite: Base attack bonus +1.  
Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on your attack roll when you fire or throw a ranged weapon at a flat-footed opponent.  
Special: A fighter may select Bowslinger as one of his fighter bonus feats.
