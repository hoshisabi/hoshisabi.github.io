# Allowed Books

This campaign originally started with only the core books being allowed. This was mainly because Dan had just had his D&D collection stolen and was in the process of buying them back. Now that much of that has been replaced, we're adding new books pretty regularly.

Currently, the following books are allowed entirely:

  * Players Handbook
  * Dungeon Master's Guide
  * Monster Manual
  * Miniatures Handbook
  * Players Handbook II
  * Complete Adventurer
  * Complete Arcane
  * Complete Warrior
  * Complete Divine
  * Complete Mage
  * Complete Scoundrel



These books are being evaluated to be included:

  * Arms and Equipment Guide
  * Unearthed Arcana
