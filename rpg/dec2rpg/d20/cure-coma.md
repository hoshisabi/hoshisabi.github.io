# Cure Coma

Cure Coma  
---  
School | Conjuration (Healing)  
Level | Brd 3, Clr 3, Drd 3, Pal 4, Rgr 4  
Casting Time | 10 minutes  
Range | Touch  
Target | Creature touched  
Duration | Instantaneous  
Saving Throw | Will (harmless)  
Spell Resistance | Yes (harmless)  
  
Cure coma will remove the _coma condition_ from a creature. This is a new condition for this campaign and covered in the [coma rules](</coma-rules>).
