# Ranged Threat

**Ranged Threat [General, Fighter]**

**Prerequisites:**  
Base Attack Bonus +6  
Combat Reflexes  
Point Blank Shot  
Precise Shot

**Description**  
If a creature within 15' of you takes an action that provokes an Attack of Opportunity, you may make a single ranged attack against  
him/her/it at your highest attack bonus. This consumes all of your remaining Attacks of Opportunity for the round.
