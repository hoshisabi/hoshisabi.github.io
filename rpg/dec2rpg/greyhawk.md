# Greyhawk

# This game has been cancelled.

The Tuesday game is set in the country of Furyondy. The characters are all natives of either Furyondy or somewhere nearby.

Presently, the campaign is taking place inside of the borders of the evil empire of Iuz in a small keep built by invading members of the Furyondian military. King Belvour of Furyondy has declared that adventurers would be free to carry home anything that they could pillage from the lands of Iuz, only paying a small tax upon their return home.

The players are currently staying inside of the keep, assisting them in clearing out a nearby cavern complex from humanoids.

I will use the official [Greyhawk deities](<http://furyondy.com/downloads/LG_Deities_v2_0.pdf>).
