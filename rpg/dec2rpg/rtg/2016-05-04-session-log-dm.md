# 2016 05 04 Session Log Dm

Players present: Joey, Paul, Chloe, Will, Rodney, Keith, Stephanie, Kat

We started this session with a continuation of the previous session's fight. The violet fungi hit hard, but it had few hitpoints, so they managed to finish them off.

When they finally passed that section, they saw it open into another large cavern, and sitting cross legged, with shut eyes was a female gnome, surrounded by various mushrooms (including what were obviously more shriekers.)

They negotiated with the gnome who agreed to fill the vial if they brought back one of the bowls from Tink's place, explaining that in order to get the bowl out of the place, they'd need to experiment with the control panel.
