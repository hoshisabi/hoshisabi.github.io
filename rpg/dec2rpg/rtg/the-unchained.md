Base Unchained:  
+1.5 **Living Construct.** Even though you were constructed, you are a living creature. You are immune to disease. You do not need to eat or breathe, but you can ingest food and drink if you wish. Instead of sleeping, you enter an inactive state for 4 hours each day. You do not dream in this state; you are fully aware of your surroundings and notice approaching enemies and other events as normal.  
+2.0 **Ability Score Increase.** You gain a +2 to Constitution.  
+0.0 **Integrated Armor.** You cannot wear armor without modifications. See Integrated Armement for details on the modifications.  
+0.5 **Integrated Armament.** During a short rest with appropriate tools, you can modify armor, tools, one-handed weapons, a shield, or an arcane focus to become part of your body. You gain no benefit from your Natural Armor trait while using armor in this fashion. This item can only be removed from your form with a similar procedure with the appropriate tools. This can't be done from you unwillingly unless you are incapacitated. You can only have one item attached to each hand in this fashion, and as a bonus action you can store or retrieve the item.

There exists two varieties of unchained, the large and powerful Stone Unchained and the small and quick Wood Unchained.

MISCELLANEOUS NOTES:  
The Unchained are not immune to the sleep spell or other similar effects, they instead go into a "unpowered" state which works the same as sleep amongst other humanoids.
