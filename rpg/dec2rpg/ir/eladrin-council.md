# Eladrin Council

![rapier.png](http://dec2rpg.wdfiles.com/local--files/ir:eladrin-council/rapier.png)

The Eladrin Council is made up of all of the eladrin who stayed behind to govern the human protectorate. Originally, they were part of the Fey Conclave, but they have come to appreciate the position of power and luxuries afforded them as members of the Eladrin Council.

Three Adjectives and Real World counterpart:

  * refined
  * swashbuckling
  * corrupt
  * Culture with Three Musketeers / Scarlet Pimpernel flavor



Power Sources:  
The elves primarily focus on arcane and martial sources, with some who use primal.

There are no known practitioners of divine, shadow, or psionic magic.
