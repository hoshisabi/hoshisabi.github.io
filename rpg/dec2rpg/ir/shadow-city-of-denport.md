# Shadow City Of Denport

![underworld.png](http://dec2rpg.wdfiles.com/local--files/ir:shadow-city-of-denport/underworld.png)

The Shadow City of Denport are the human resistance in the elven protectorate of Denport. Mainly controlled by the Cult of Eshvara Nox, they've been organizing the humans and using events to their advantage to undermine the power of the elven governors of Denport.

Three Adjectives and Real World counterpart:

  * hidden
  * sage-like
  * manipulative
  * culture with a Illuminati / secret society flavor
