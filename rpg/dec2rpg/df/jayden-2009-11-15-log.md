# Jayden's Log: 2009 11 15

I've perfected "The Jayden"; my signature telegraphic, all-out, called shot to the eye move. It's a beautiful thing to behold. We haven't encountered the bandit leader again but we found fire and air elementals. I'm finding my grenades are playthings. The environment the air mephits are from is astounding. We found the what we think is the water elementals but we did not engage them. We camped outside and were attacked by several birds with royal plumage I hope to sell, as I plan to invest in Giant Spider Silk to increase my DR before we head to the keep to uncover the plan.
