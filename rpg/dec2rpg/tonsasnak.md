# Tonsasnak

Tonsasnak is a campaign being run on Sundays by Dave Ditsch. In the campaign, all of the players are playing kobolds. The rules were proposed as "munchkin", and as such we're all playing with a lot of the alternate rules that are typically considered unbalanced. (Gestalt, firearms, etc).

Here's some more pages with more information about the world: [history](</tonsasnak:history>), [races](</tonsasnak:races>)
