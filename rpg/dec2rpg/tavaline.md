# Tavaline

Tavaline is the name of the continent and the campaign of the alternate campaign that hasn't started yet. 

Some [questions and answers about this campaign](</tavaline-questions-and-answers>)

Just as a note, the name is derived from the Estonian word for mundane. I needed a word, so I did a [wiktionary search](<http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/mundane>) for "mundane" and picked the one that sounded the nicest.
